<Steps>
    <Step title="Fork the Bigbytes GitHub repo">
    Visit [GitHub](https://github.com/getbigbytes/bigbytes) and fork the Bigbytes repo, then clone it to your machine.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Create a branch">
    Create a branch in your forked repo and commit your changes.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Create a pull request">
    Once you've [made and tested your changes](/contributing/development-environment), create a [pull request](https://docs.github.com/en/pull-requests/collaborating-with-pull-requests/proposing-changes-to-your-work-with-pull-requests/creating-a-pull-request-from-a-fork) on the Bigbytes repo.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Tag Bigbytes team members for a review">
    Add members for review in GitHub, please tag one of the following: 
        - `@getbigbytes`
    </Step>
</Steps>
